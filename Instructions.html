Footsie is a game for two hands, ideally belonging to two different people. Here's how to play:

foo Start by touching and holding the angry faces in the corners of the screen. One player should place their first two fingers on the purple pulsing faces on the left side while the other player places their fingers on the orange pulsing faces.
bar Each turn, one face will go to sleep and another will wake up angry. The sleeping face will pulse outward to show you can take your finger off of it. Move the finger to the new angry face, pulsing inward. Don't move your other fingers!
bar Sometimes two faces will go to sleep at the same time, causing two other faces to wake up angry. Both players will have to move one of their fingers to reach the angry face pulsing in their color.
bar If a finger comes off of a happy face for too long, the game is over. You earn one point for each successful turn. Work together to get a high score!
bar The game will pause if you get a phone call, a "low battery" warning, or some other system alert. If this happens, put your fingers back in position on the angry faces to continue the game.
